Stage 1: Car Talk

  My buddy's car has started behaving weirdly and I'm not quite sure what's gone wrong with it. I told him you're pretty experienced with cars, so he's sent me the following text describing the symptoms his car has been having. Do you know what's gone bad?

Yeah, so the other day it stalled and left me stranded when I stopped at the intersection of Forbes and Morewood. Before that, I thought I might have heard some backfiring and the car was running all weird. It was really jerky and was idling really rough. I probably should have pulled over earlier, but I was trying to make it to the gas station nearby after realizing that my gas was pretty low. It may just be my faulty memory, but I'm pretty sure I filled up the gas tank way too recently to have to fill it up again, and it caught me off guard.
