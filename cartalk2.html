Stage 5: Car Talk Part 2

  I hope you liked those Car Talk puzzlers, because you'll have to solve one to advance to the next stage! You may want to grab a pencil a paper for this one! This doozy of a puzzler comes from Terry Sare, but I've adjusted it a bit. Terry writes,

  I was driving on the highway the other day recently and I happened to notice my odometer. Like most odometers nowadays, it shows six digits in whole miles -- no tenths of a mile. At the time, my car had miles in the hundred thousands, so my odometer was showing 6 digits with no leading zeros.

  Now, what I saw that day was very interesting. I noticed that the last 4 digits formed a palindrome. Even more interesting, one mile later, the last 5 numbers were palindromic. Another mile later, the middle 4 digits of 6 were palindromic. Now, this may seem unbelievable, but one mile later, all 6 digits formed a palindrome!

  Assuming I'm not a liar, how many miles were on my odometer when I first looked at it?
